ABOUT TECHANNZA

The most awaited Inter college event Teachannza 2k24 is here now! TechANNza, the intercollegiate IT fest organized by the Department of Computer Science, Yantra Club , ignites a competitive spirit among students while providing a dynamic platform for showcasing their technical prowess. This year's edition promises an exciting lineup of six events spanning both technical and non-technical domains, offering participants a diverse range of challenges and opportunities for growth. Join us as we come together to celebrate innovation, collaboration, and the boundless possibilities of technology.
